Size Guide

We never want to send you something that doesn't fit properly. Please use these size charts to help determine your size. Size guide provide you international size conversion, whatever from UK, Europe or US. Most of size on our website is manually measure; there might have deviation in 2 cm which depending on the different fabric. If you have a specific sizing question or would like more information, please contact our customer service department.

How to measure:

a) Chest: Measure your chest over the fullest part of your bust while wearing a bra
b) Under bust: If you're looking for a bra you should take another measure directly under the bust
c) Waist: measure around natural waistline
d) Seat: measure at widest part
e) Inside leg length: measure from top of inside leg at crotch to ankle bone
